Sound Sleep

The World of Insomnia

Sound Sleep
Best Silent Film
Independent Talent
Intl. Film Festival
2018

What will happen when you develop a strange condition which leads to insomnia? This silent short film is a story of a woman who has difficulty sleeping; find out how she gets what she wants most in her life — The Sound Sleep!

We know that you are eagerly waiting to watch this award-winning short-film but as per official rules of many short film festivals, we can't release the film publicly yet. We will release the film shortly. Please like our Sound-Sleep Facebook Page for updates and notifications about events and release information. Here is a list of accolades we have received so far:

  • Best Silent Film, Special Jury, Independent Talent International Film Festival - 2018

Official Selections:

  • LIFFT India Filmotsav, World Cine Fest, Lonavla - 2018
  • JIFF - Jaipur International Film festival - 2019
  • Europa Film Festival Barcelona, Spain - 2018
  • Goa Short Film Festival - 2018
  • Hawaii Theatre Centre, 3 North America Teaser Future Film Festival - 2018
  • Free Spirit Film Festival - Himachal Pradesh, India - 2018
  • El Paso, TX IMPACTFest Film Festival - 2018
  • Trichy Film Festival - 2018

We believe in telling intriguing stories

We believe in making concept-oriented films with strong content that can resonate with millions around the globe. This story explores the effects and impact of insomnia, a sleeplessness and an interesting cause to it. It's a story worth telling.

Your support has been a huge boost

Funding from our backers has enabled us to submit our 10 minute short film to hundreds of short film festivals across the globe. We're eternally grateful!
